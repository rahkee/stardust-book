Introducing "Stardust: A Journey to Remember”, a poetic and enchanting story that follows an adventurer named Naledi on an epic journey through inner and outer universes to remember the miracle it is to be alive.

This journey reminds us that the most challenging times can provide some of life's greatest gifts, and that these challenges build our character and help us to shine bright!

This book is created for dreamers of all ages. Naledi is a mirror for the child in us all!

The thoughtful messaging and whimsical characters aim to nurture positivity, imagination, and core life values in children, while simultaneously reminding parents and all adults of the greatness inside each and every one of us.

Our playful cadence and impactful words are fun to read, and our art will spark the imagination of all who see.

We want everyone to have access to the message and art in this book. It is both a gift to our inner child, and a gift to our children and future children. This book is created with the intention to be a positive impact for generations to come!

The Back Story

On a beautiful spring day in Arizona, a journaling session led me to acknowledge the lessons that were gifted to me from my greatest challenges. In that moment of clarity, a little whisper nudged me to write a poem, which became “Stardust.”

“Stardust” showed up as a reflection of my own life. It was a call to action, urging me to start sharing the music deep inside me. I had poems that were sitting dormant in my journal for years, and I began sharing my work online and at open mics, leading to speaking invitations from conferences around the country.

The feedback resonated with a shared desire: "We want these poems for our children."

One morning, I awoke from a dream visualizing “Stardust” as a children's book. Eagerly, I noted every detail. Weeks before this, I'd met Daniel Fleck, an incredibly creative artist. Seeing this as serendipitous, I approached him about collaborating, and after seeing his initial sketches and beautiful interpretation of Stardust, we KNEW that we had to create SOMETHING together.

With my niece Yara's birth, dear friends having children, and my niece and nephew moving close, I was inspired to create something special for them.

Daniel shared that in lieu of having children of his own yet, he aspired to share the love that would occupy that space with the world; an act of service that could benefit current and future generations. This project aligned perfectly. It felt divinely appointed, as if we were selected to be honored conduits of these messages.

We committed to monthly catch-ups, sharing life updates and book progress. After a year, our labor of love, "Stardust", is now ready to make its debut. Trust, authenticity, and a shared vision fueled our journey, and we hope it instills the same values in those who experience "Stardust.”
